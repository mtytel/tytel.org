Info

I'm a San Francisco software synthesizer developer. I believe in free software and most of what I work on is open source. I've started a synth devblog to record and share my experiences making music software.

I make computer-aided art and music where I splice pieces of software generated riffs and phrases into full poems and songs. For other art I use a lot of physics structures and simulations from cloth to water to centipedes to flowers.
