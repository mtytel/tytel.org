Playground

I was the main developer and organizer for the app Playground which is shipped with Leap Motion's V2 Software. By putting toy robot heads on their bodies and by picking petals off a flower it should teach someone how to use the Leap Motion Controller. It's made in Unity using the Leap Motion V2 Unity Assets and is open source.
